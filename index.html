Why us?

Greek Yogurt is healthier than regular yogurt.
It's made with live active cultures and is an excellent source of calcium for healthy bones.

It's high in protein and low in sugar, which makes it perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner! Greek yogurt is made by straining out the whey from regular yogurt.
This results in a thicker, creamier product that's higher in protein and lower in sugar than regular yogurt. Greek yogurt is also much more versatile than regular yogurt. It can be used in a variety of recipes, from breakfast dishes to desserts.
